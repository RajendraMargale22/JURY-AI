    Did You Know? Legal Facts

    Constitution of India

    The Indian Constitution is the longest written constitution in the world, with 395 articles and 12 schedules.

    Supreme Court

    The Supreme Court of India was established on January 26, 1950, and is the highest judicial authority in the country.

    High Courts

    India has 25 High Courts, with the Calcutta High Court being the oldest, established in 1862.

    Legal System

    India follows a common law system based on recorded judicial precedents, inherited from the British legal system.
